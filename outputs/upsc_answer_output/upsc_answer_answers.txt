Q: The ultimate measure of a man is not where he stands in the moment of comfort & convenience, but where he stands at times of challenge & controversy.

Siddharth Gautama was born in Lumbini. During his birth many sages were invited to bless the child. While many of them blessed him with great & luxurious life one of the astrologers predicted that either he will be a Great King or a Monk.

This prediction disturbed his father who never wanted Siddharth to be a monk. That day he decided that he will provide all the comfort, convenience & wealth to his son, and Siddharth was never allowed to leave the palace.

One night, Gautama decided to go beyond the walls of palace, so he went out on his horse "Kantak" while he was out he saw a sick man, a saint and a dead body.
